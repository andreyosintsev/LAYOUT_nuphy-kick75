Детали и особенности

Virtual side of a physical switch. The Win/Mac mode switch, an integral part of all NuPhy keyboards, is now fully integrated with VIA's layers to make it more extensible and customizable, with the same convenience.

nSA vs mSA: Tailored Typing Experiences. Our keyboards feature both nSA and mSA keycap profiles to suit different needs. The nSA, with low-profile switches, is sleek and ideal for compact setups, while the mSA, with high-profile switches, offers improved ergonomics and larger surfaces for a more comfortable typing experience.

* The above battery capacity is for the NuPhyIO version. The QMK version has a battery capacity of 4000mAh.

Long-Lasting Battery Life. Equipped with a 2500mAh battery, the Kick75 delivers up to 5 weeks of regular use on a single charge: 36.7 days with 8 hours of daily use without backlight, 24.5 days with 12 hours of daily use without backlight, 7.6 days with 8 hours of daily use with backlight, and 5.1 days with 12 hours of daily use with backlight.

Seamless Multi-Mode Connectivity. The Kick75 offers a blazing-fast 1000Hz polling rate in 2.4G wireless mode, ensuring ultra-responsive performance for gaming or fast-paced typing. With three versatile connection modes—2.4G wireless, Bluetooth, and USB-C wired—it adapts seamlessly to various scenarios, whether you're working on multiple devices, gaming competitively, or simply enjoying a smooth and reliable typing experience.

Часто задаваемые вопросы

If Kick75 supports both low-profile and high-profile switches, why can’t I switch profiles right after purchase?

Kick 75 supports both profiles by design, buteach keyboard is configured as either low-profile or high-profile a purchase.

To switch profiles later, a dedicated conversion kit is required.

Which conversion kit should I choose?

Choose based on how you use your keyboard.

If you prioritize typing comfort and a stable desk setup, switching to a high-profile configuration with silent switches is ideal for long working sessions.If portability matters more and you frequently carry your keyboard between locations, a low-profile configuration offers a lighter, more compact experience.

No matter which profile you choose, Kick75 is designed to perform equally well for both work and entertainment.

What should I consider when purchasing Kick75?

You will need to choose between High-Profile or Low-Profile switches when purchasing.

If you wish to switch between the two, you can purchase the Profile Swap Kit accessory to interchange the profiles.

What versions are available for Kick75?

You can choose between the NuPhy IO version or the QMK version when purchasing.

Does Kick75 support 3D printing customization?

We provide 3D printing files that allow users to customize their keyboards based on personal preferences.

For example, the green feet at the bottom of the keyboard can be removed and replaced with 3D-printed parts in other colors.

When will the STP files be available? ?

The STP files will be released on March 20. During this period, we will conduct prototyping tests on these files and notify everyone through the community and email.
